Process Overview

  • Step 1 - Initial Answer: You will review a clinical question with multiple-choice options. You will select the answer you think is most appropriate and indicate your confidence level (0-1).
  • Step 2 - After AI's Analysis: You will see the AI agent's clinical reasoning and analysis. You may then revise your answer selection and confidence level, or keep your original choice.
  • Step 3 - After Correct Answer: You will see the correct answer. You may make final revisions to your answer selection and confidence level based on this information.
  • Step 1 - Initial Treatment Plan: You will review the full clinical context for a patient case. You will provide your treatment recommendations in three categories:
    • Medications (with confidence level 0-1)
    • Procedures (with confidence level 0-1)
    • Diagnoses (with confidence level 0-1)
  • Step 2 - After Agent's Recommendation: You will see the AI agent's treatment recommendation. You may then revise your treatment plan and confidence levels for each category, or keep your original inputs.
  • Step 3 - After Actual Treatment: You will see the actual treatment outcomes that were administered. You may make final revisions to your treatment plan and confidence levels for each category based on this information.
