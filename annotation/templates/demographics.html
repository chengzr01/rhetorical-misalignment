Process Overview

  • Step 1 - Initial Answer: You will review a clinical question with multiple-choice options. You will select the answer you think is most appropriate and indicate your confidence level (0-1).
  • Step 2 - After AI's Analysis: You will see the AI agent's clinical reasoning and analysis. You may then revise your answer selection and confidence level, or keep your original choice.
  • Step 3 - After Correct Answer: You will see the correct answer. You may make final revisions to your answer selection and confidence level based on this information.
